About Me
关于我

A caring companion for your whole journey

专业陪伴，温暖同行

My name is Yaling Song, and I have been a professional postpartum caregiver and newborn specialist for over 10 years. Born in Weihai, Shandong, China in 1975, my gentle nature, reliability, and genuine love for babies and new mothers are at the heart of everything I do.

我叫宋亚玲，1975年出生于山东威海，从事月嫂和育儿嫂工作已超过10年。我性格温柔善良、踏实麻利、干净利落，对宝宝和产妈妈充满爱心与耐心。

I have cared for families across China, the United Kingdom, and the United States — including New York, Austin, Los Angeles, San Francisco Bay Area, and Phoenix, Arizona. I am fully comfortable serving both Mandarin-speaking and English-speaking households.

我曾服务的家庭遍及中国、英国（伦敦）及美国（纽约、奥斯汀、洛杉矶、旧金山湾区、凤凰城），能够为中英文家庭提供无障碍的贴心服务。

Beyond baby care, I hold a Level 5 Chinese Pastry Chef certification and prepare nourishing postpartum meals — soups, traditional breads, and balanced dishes — to support milk supply and recovery.

除了专业护理，我还拥有五级中式面点师资格，能为产妈妈精心准备营养月子餐，包括汤品、馒头、肉夹馍等，确保奶水充足、身体恢复。
